Constantina "Connie" Scoullis

Hello World!

My name is Connie, and I am a software engineer based in the San Francisco Bay Area. I graduated from Rutgers University in 2017, where I double majored in Computer Science and Theatre Arts. I find code to be one of the closest things to magic we have, and I am particulary passionate about user interface development and user experience design.

Currently, I work as a software engineer at Cisco. I've also worked at iCIMS and Viacom Media Networks, and before graduating from Rutgers, I lead the marketing efforts for Cabaret Theatre, HackHERS, and HackRU.

I am looking for new opportunities in fullstack or frontend development, as well as freelance work. Feel free to check out some of my stuff below, and if you're interested in working together feel free to email me.
